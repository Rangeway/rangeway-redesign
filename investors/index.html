The thesis

Experience changes the category.

01

An open category

Public charging is commonly designed around the vehicle. Rangeway competes on the experience of the people inside it.

02

Dwell time, reconsidered

Time at a charger can support useful amenities, stronger loyalty, and a differentiated roadside brand.

03

Experience as the moat

Hardware and electricity can commoditize. Consistently operating a place that feels hospitable is harder to reproduce.

04

Three formats, one standard

Waystation, Basecamp, and Summit adapt the same hospitality promise to regional routes, major corridors, and destinations.
