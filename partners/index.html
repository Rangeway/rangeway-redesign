Partners

Built with the best, hosted by the best.

Rangeway is a brand and an operating company. We work with hospitality operators who host us on their properties, and with technology partners who build the systems behind the experience.

For hospitality operators

Host a Rangeway on your property.

Rangeway sites are designed to belong on hospitality properties: flagship resorts, lodges, boutique inns, and the corridors that connect them. If you operate a property that fits the brand and want to host a Rangeway, we would like to hear about it.

Tell us about the property: location, size, the kind of guests it serves, and what brought Rangeway to mind. We read every inquiry.

Technology and infrastructure partners

The systems behind the experience.

Structural and Building

  • LoüTeq

    Structural and building partner

    Every canopy, lounge, and supporting building across the network is built from LoüTeq's Structural Engineered Bamboo, a carbon-negative material stronger than steel and rapidly renewable. BMW and Rivian use the same system.

    Visit LoüTeq

Energy and Infrastructure

  • Carbon DC

    Energy infrastructure

    Carbon DC is Rangeway's strategic partner on off-grid sites, where solar generation, battery storage, and DC fast charging integrate into a single architecture that operates independently of the utility. At every Summit, Carbon DC powers every Lookout, charger, and clubhouse off-grid.

    Visit Carbon DC

  • Ekoenergetyka

    Ultra-fast charging hardware

    Ekoenergetyka brings European engineering to the chargers at the heart of the driver experience. The hardware delivers performance, service life, and a design language worthy of a hospitality-forward site.

    Visit Ekoenergetyka

  • Electric Era

    Charging hardware and storage

    Electric Era's RetailEdge dispensers bring fast, reliable charging to select Rangeway sites, backed by integrated battery storage that keeps every session quick and consistent.

    Visit Electric Era

  • HyWatts

    Modular off-grid power systems

    HyWatts builds Power Plant-in-a-Box systems that integrate solar, battery storage, and hydrogen-ready generation. Rangeway brings HyWatts in on a project-by-project basis when the grid is slow, expensive, or unavailable.

    Visit HyWatts

Software and Driver Experience

  • ChargeMate

    24/7 AI driver support

    ChargeMate is the always-on concierge behind every Rangeway charger. AI-led troubleshooting handles most issues immediately and escalates to the Rangeway team when needed, so no driver is ever stuck.

    Visit ChargeMate

  • Juice

    Plug-and-charge technology

    Juice powers seamless authentication and session handling at every charger. Drivers arrive, plug in, and go, without apps or friction.

    Visit Juice

  • Monta

    Charge point management

    Monta is the operational backbone of the network. The platform handles payments, roaming, uptime monitoring, and the infrastructure that keeps every charger connected and every transaction clean.

    Visit Monta

  • WattsUp

    Predictive maintenance

    WattsUp handles the quiet work that keeps every charger reliable. The platform reads charger telemetry to catch failures before drivers ever feel them, keeping uptime at the level hospitality demands.

    Visit WattsUp

Hospitality and Amenities

  • Pebble

    Mobile driver lounges

    Pebble provides the mobile lounges at new Rangeway sites, climate-controlled spaces where drivers and guests can relax, work, and recharge while their vehicle does the same.

    Visit Pebble

  • Rivian

    Food and coffee

    Rivian supplies the upfitted electric vans that serve as Rangeway's on-site food trucks at early sites, run by local operators bringing fresh food and coffee.

    Visit Rivian
