The people behind Rangeway.

Every decision in this company is made by someone who has run a real property for real guests, or driven a real EV on a real road trip. Usually both.

Team

The people behind the network.

  • Zak Winnick

    Founder and CEO

    Zak has spent his career operating luxury resorts, wellness retreats, and flagship urban hotels. He is also a longtime EV driver, community organizer, and podcast host, with firsthand insight into what charging actually feels like on a long trip.

    LinkedIn

  • Luke Schüette

    Director of Design and Construction

    Luke leads design and construction for the Rangeway network. A structural systems innovator, Luke chairs ASTM's task group on Structural Engineered Bamboo and holds patents in solar charging structures. He has designed for industry leaders like BMW, Rivian, and Tesla. His solar canopies and lounge structures define the look of every Rangeway destination.

    LinkedIn

  • Theo Reichgelt

    Brand and Growth

    Theo founded Nexxt Industry, Europe's leading eMobility marketing agency, and specializes in charging network brand development and market positioning. He also hosts The eMobility Marketing Podcast.

    LinkedIn

  • James Regan

    Finance and Strategy

    James has built his career in corporate development and business analytics, and serves as Managing Partner at CapitalWorks. He specializes in infrastructure project financing and capital strategy for the buildout of the Rangeway network.

    LinkedIn

  • Stephanie McGreevy

    Policy and Public Capital

    Stephanie's career has been in zero-emission vehicle infrastructure, and she is the founder of Open Energy Alliance. She specializes in LCFS optimization and grant funding strategies, including NEVI and state-level incentive programs.

    LinkedIn
