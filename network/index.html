The road gets better at the stop.

Rangeway is building an experience system for electric travel: fast charging wrapped in indoor comfort, useful time, and a clear return to the road.

Compare the three formats

The operating promise

Built around the traveler, not only the charger.

  1. 01

    Comfort indoors

    A climate-controlled place to sit, work, regroup, and use a clean restroom.

  2. 02

    Useful time

    Wi-Fi, food appropriate to the format, and space shaped around a real travel day.

  3. 03

    Forward momentum

    A legible arrival and an easy transition back to the route once the stop is complete.

Rangeway standard 001

Indoor Comfort Guarantee

Every Rangeway format is designed around access to climate-controlled indoor comfort. The footprint and service model change, but the traveler is never treated as an accessory to the charging equipment.
