The Network

Three formats. One unwavering standard.

Each Rangeway location is shaped by its corridor and community, and held to the same hospitality standard across the network.

Why three formats

Hospitality scales with the road.

A regional route deserves a Waystation. A high-traffic run deserves a Basecamp. A weekend belongs at a Summit. The scale shifts. The standard does not.

Inside the network

The Indoor Comfort Guarantee.

Every Waystation and Basecamp commits to climate-controlled indoor comfort, with comfortable restrooms, generous seating, and reliable high-speed Wi-Fi. The lounge is part of the standard, not an upsell.

At Summits, the hospitality extends further still, into overnight stays in modular Lookouts.

The formats

One standard, three expressions.

Each format delivers the same hospitality promise at a scale that fits the corridor.

Streamlined comfort

Waystation

Ultra-fast charging paired with a climate-controlled driver's lounge, comfortable restrooms, generous seating, and high-speed Wi-Fi.
