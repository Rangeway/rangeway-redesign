The human layer

Trailkeepers turn a stop into hospitality.

Basecamp is staffed. Trailkeepers support the day around the charging session: welcoming travelers, caring for shared spaces, and helping the stop run as a place rather than a row of equipment.

The role is intentionally service-oriented. Equipment, hours, and staffing details remain specific to each public project.
