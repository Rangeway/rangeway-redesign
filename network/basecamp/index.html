What a Basecamp is

Hospitality at full scale.

Basecamp is what happens when we stop apologizing for charging stops. It is an actual destination, staffed by people who know your name before you leave, placed on corridors where the traffic earns the full experience.

Everything we have learned about hospitality at flagship properties shows up here. The cafe is real, the service is real, and the comfort is real. The chargers happen to be excellent too.

What you'll find

A staffed, hospitality-led experience.

Ultra-fast charging

Flagship-scale capacity for the highest-traffic corridors, designed for steady throughput and peak weekends alike.

Staffed driver's lounge

Our most generous indoor space, with seating zones for work, for family, and for quiet.

Staffed cafe

A real kitchen with a real menu, local coffee, warm food, and hospitality in the room.

Trailkeepers on site

Hospitality staff trained to help, not just to supervise, with a difference that's felt the moment you walk in.

Extended hours

Built for the full arc of the day, from early mornings to late nights to long holiday weekends.

Amenities for families and travelers

Play corners for kids, nursing rooms, and pet-friendly patios that turn a stop into the kind our guests recommend.

One step further

For the journey that should last overnight, there is Summit.

Modular Lookouts, a central clubhouse, and fully off-grid solar charging, where the stop becomes the trip.
