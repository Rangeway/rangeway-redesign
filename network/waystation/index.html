What a Waystation is

Where the network finds its rhythm.

The Waystation is Rangeway at steady state. A location that can hold a regional route on its own, with enough capacity for a busy afternoon and enough comfort for the longest stops.

Every Waystation honors the Indoor Comfort Guarantee, with a climate-controlled driver's lounge, comfortable restrooms, and high-speed Wi-Fi as the baseline of the experience.

What you'll find

The full driver's lounge experience.

Ultra-fast charging

A sensible array for regional and interstate routes, sized to keep a steady flow without ever feeling industrial.

Climate-controlled driver's lounge

Comfortable, considered space that's warm in winter, cool in summer, and welcoming year-round.

Comfortable restrooms

Clean, well-lit, and well-stocked, the kind you'd find at a good hotel lobby.

Comfortable seating

Soft chairs, work-friendly surfaces, and thoughtful lighting that make it a place to actually settle in.

High-speed Wi-Fi

Reliable enough to take a meeting on, and open to every driver.

Local coffee, snacks, and provisions

A curated pantry of local goods, with the kind of small details that turn a stop into a moment.
