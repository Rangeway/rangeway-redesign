A self-directed stop

Four moves.
No service theater.

The model is designed to make the path from arrival to departure legible without relying on an on-site team.

  1. 01Arrive

    A compact footprint keeps the transition off the route straightforward.

  2. 02Charge

    The charging session anchors the visit; equipment details remain project-specific.

  3. 03Reset indoors

    Climate control, clean restrooms, seating, Wi-Fi, and local provisions support the pause.

  4. 04Return

    The visit ends with a simple move back to the road.

Indoor comfortEvery format

Essential hospitality

The baseline is a better place to wait.

Waystation compresses Rangeway’s indoor-comfort standard into the smallest format. It is not a staffed cafe or destination stay; it is the practical reset many routes are missing.

  • Climate-controlled interior
  • Clean restrooms
  • Seating and Wi-Fi
  • Local provisions
