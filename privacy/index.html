Rangeway Energy, Inc. ("Rangeway," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website at rangeway.co and related subdomains (collectively, the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you provide directly or that is generated through ordinary website analytics.

Information you provide:

  • Contact form submissions: When you submit the contact form, we collect your name, email address, interest category, and message.
  • Field Notes newsletter subscriptions: When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. Field Notes is operated through Buttondown, a third-party newsletter platform.

Information collected automatically:

  • Analytics: We use Tinylytics, a privacy-focused analytics service, to understand how visitors use the Site. Tinylytics collects aggregated, non-identifying information such as page views, referring URLs, browser type, and coarse geographic region (for example, country or state). Tinylytics does not use cookies or fingerprinting and does not track individual users across sites.

Information we do not collect:

  • We do not use cookies.
  • We do not use advertising or retargeting trackers.
  • We do not collect financial information, account credentials, or sensitive personal information through the Site.
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To route your inquiry to the appropriate internal team (press, investor relations, partnerships, careers, or general)
  • To send you Field Notes newsletter emails if you subscribed
  • To maintain ordinary business records
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To improve the Site and understand how visitors use it (using aggregated analytics only)

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate the Site:

  • Self-hosted infrastructure: The Site is served from Rangeway-operated servers (currently a VPS with Nginx). Static assets are published via our continuous deployment pipeline.
  • Formsubmit.co: Processes contact form submissions (including a spam check) and routes them to our internal email inbox.
  • Buttondown: Operates our Field Notes newsletter. Subscriber email addresses are stored by Buttondown under their privacy practices, available at buttondown.com/privacy.
  • Tinylytics: Provides aggregated website analytics. Tinylytics' privacy practices are available at tinylytics.app.

Each of these providers processes data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With the third-party service providers listed above, as necessary to operate the Site
  • When required by law, such as in response to a subpoena, court order, or government request
  • To protect our legal rights, prevent fraud, or address security concerns
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of our assets, in which case we will notify affected users

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and newsletter subscriber information for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry, provide ongoing communications, and maintain business records. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us (see Section 9).

6. Your Rights if You Live in California

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") gives you the following rights:

  • Right to know: You may request information about the personal information we have collected about you, including the categories of information, the sources of the information, the purposes for collecting it, and the third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to delete: You may request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to correct: You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing: We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legal@rangeway.co. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

7. Your Rights if You Live in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction covered by the General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR") or UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of access to your personal information
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate information
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

The legal bases on which we process personal information under GDPR include:

  • Consent for newsletter subscriptions (you may withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing)
  • Legitimate interests for responding to contact form submissions and operating the Site
  • Legal obligation where required by law

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legal@rangeway.co. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

8. Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your continued use of the Site after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
